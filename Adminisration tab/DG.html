DIRECTOR GENERAL OF CDAC

Director Jitesh Choudhary


A committed and focused executive with comprehensive knowledge and understanding of product design, development, and project management. Presently working as, the Director in Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC, a Govt. of India R&D Organization) and heading its C-DAC Silchar Centre. Experienced Product Manager with a demonstrated history of working in Electronic Product development and Project Management. Skilled in Project Management, Product Management, Electronics Engineering, Embedded Systems(microcontrollers), Product development, Electronics Hardware Design, Firmware development, and Machine learning. Over 15 years of experience in electronics product design and development, project planning & execution for the entire product lifecycle, including systems design, requirement gathering, scheduling, budgeting, and commissioning. Developed many diversified and innovative radiation monitoring products for Nuclear Power Plants, Reprocessing Plants, and Defence Establishment. All these products, typically electronic products and developed with proprietary technologies; meet the customized requirements of Indian nuclear establishments. These products are developed with standards like IEC and ANSI to achieve quality. Indigenous products were also developed to retrofit imported instruments for these plants. Has skill to bring people together and work towards common goals. Technical knowledge and experience in successfully leading teams and managing projects led to work as a driven technical project manager for the past many years.
